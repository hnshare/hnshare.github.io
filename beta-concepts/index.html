Teaching Sarf - Ideas:

Learning tool: Sarf family:

when student comes across new unfamiliar verb, it helps to see other rhyming verbs which are on the same pattern. Demo:

    

Explanation:
In the vid, student clicks a Verb in the Qur'aan (Ista'ala - 1st example; naada, 2nd example) and sees relevant info like Sarf sagheer and rhyming verbs like iktasaba, i'tarafa etc.
Focus on the Animated verbs section; basically the verbs from same mazeed feeh baab are shown -- categorized into: salim, ajwaf, naqis, mithaal.

Guess Who game idea:

First cut version: https://hnshare.github.io/sarfi
Explanation: Both learners open this page; similar to the original Guess-Who game, they both see a number of tiles on right from which they have to guess & their own tile (highlighted in different color).
Ask your opponent question and click any eliminated cards to discard them. Whoever gueses right first, wins!

To keep track of score, there's "Did you win? Yes/No" buttons which also restarts the game. Inspired from these 2 games found online:

Does it have extra letters?
