1. Hidayatun Nahw Lesson 1 - المقدمة

         Study Notes                               Ext links: Slides    Videos: Part 1   Part 2   Part 3     
        
  • Nahw Definition: It is a science with principles with which are known the case endings of the 3 types of words where it concerns declinable and non declinable words and the method of how they are ordered with each other. The objective is to avoid mistakes in the speech of the Arabs.
    It concerns two objects: الكلمة and الكلام .
